The Mozilla Foundation: Empowering the Open Web
Introduction:

The Mozilla Foundation: A non-profit organization dedicated to promoting an open and accessible internet.

  • Established in 2003 after the success of the Mozilla open-source web browser
  • Mission: Ensure the internet remains a global public resource, open and accessible to all.
Key Objectives:
  • Championing open-source software development.
  • Defending internet privacy and security.
  • Encouraging digital literacy and web literacy.
  • Fostering open innovation and collaboration.
Mozilla Products and Projects:
  • Firefox Browser
  • Thunderbird Email Client
